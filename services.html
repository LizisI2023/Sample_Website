Services

With every pet, comes responsibility.

We do ask that you are 18 or older, have a stable home with a proof of residence, and can financially care for the dog. Every dog has different needs, but love is number. We do ask that you have a vet and stay up to date on shots.

We offer spay and nueter services for cats. Fow low-income families, it will be offered for a reduced price.

For Dogs

We offer spay and nueter services for dogs. Fow low-income families, it will be offered for a reduced price. We also offer rabies shots for $10.
